Nova Constructions is a Company Like no Other!

It's a company that represents more than half a century of hard work, exceptional quality, innovative thinking, competitive decisions and the strong desire to drive the economy, while leaving a lasting legacy. Their tremendous philanthropic contributions to the wider community are legendary and the fierce loyalty they inspire in their employees is a true testament to the vision of the company and on that is unparalleled in any industry.

In 2006, Nova Constructions announced its return to the world of racing, with the grand reopening the Riverside International Speedway. The entire track was refurbished to make it not just the best track in Canada, but one of the best short tracks in the world. Nova Construction provided a world-class racing facility, the only NASCAR sanctioned track east of Quebec and put Nova Scotia on the motorsport map.
