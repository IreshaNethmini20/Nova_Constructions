Frequently Asked Questions




Q.

What is a Construction Management System (CMS)?

A.

A Construction Management System is a software designed to help construction professionals manage projects, resources, documents, and communication effectively (CMS)

Q.

How can a Construction Management System (CMS) benefit your construction project?

A.

A CMS can streamline project management, improve collaboration, track project progress, manage budgets, and reduce errors, ultimately leading to cost savings and better project outcomes.

Q.

What features should you look for in a Construction Management System (CMS) ?

A.

Look for features like project scheduling, budget tracking, document management, communication tools, resource allocation, reporting, and mobile accessibility
